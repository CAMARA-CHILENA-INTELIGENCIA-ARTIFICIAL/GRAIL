Key Insights

What this batch reveals about graph-enhanced retrieval

+1.60

Biggest win: Q09

GRAIL 4.85 vs RAG 3.25. The WHO+WHAT+TERMS formula matched entity descriptions for "proceso de elaboración de garantías explícitas basado en estudios epidemiológicos" — finding criteria that RAG's chunk search missed entirely.

+1.25

Structural advantage: Q10

GRAIL 4.10 vs RAG 2.85. The entity graph connects the Fund entity to its four funding sources (donaciones, cooperación, rentabilidad). RAG returned a generic answer about FONASA without specific sources.

local

local_search is the star

Used in 6/10 questions, averaging 4.70 weighted score. The entity-description matching with retrieval queries bridges patient language to legal concepts better than raw chunk cosine.

mixed

Tool combinations work

Q06 and Q10 used local+cascade — local found the concept entities, cascade filled in specific text details. This multi-tool strategy is unique to GRAIL and unavailable to single-tool RAG.
